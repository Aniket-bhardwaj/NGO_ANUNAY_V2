ABOUT US

Satya Mission NGO, founded by Colonel Satyavrat Bhardwaj, is dedicated to social welfare, education, and environmental conservation. The organization focuses on empowering marginalized communities by providing access to education, healthcare, and resources for sustainable living. Through various projects like tree plantations, clothing donations, and elder care, Satya Mission aims to create a better future for all. Guided by the legacy of Guru Shanti Swaroop Ji and Shreemati Lakshmi Devi, the NGO works relentlessly to bring positive change and inspire hope.

OUR PROJECTS

Happiness Drive

On 20th July, Satya Shree Lakshmi Shanti Sansthan visited Silver Lining Old Age Home in Dwarka for a happiness drive. The team engaged elders with games, songs, and conversations. They gifted potted plants, handmade cards, and fresh fruits, creating a joyful and meaningful experience for everyone involved.

Plantation Drive

On July 16, 2024, Satya Shree Lakshmi Shanti Sansthan held a tree plantation drive at Moksha Dham, Dwarka, enhancing the area's beauty. Thanks to our volunteers and Mr. Charanjit Singh Majithia for their support!

Clothing Drive

On April 9, 2024, Satya Shree Lakshmi Shanti Sansthan organized a clothing drive in Basti Dwarka, providing essential clothing to over 50 individuals in need. The event showcased community unity, generosity, and the impact of collective action.
